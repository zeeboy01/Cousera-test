About Prof Maqari

  • Prof. Ahmad Ibrahim Maqari, was born on 15th September 1976, in Zaria LGA Kaduna State,Nigeria.


  • Prof Maqari started his primary Education in Katsina state of Nigeria in 1987, Sheikh Maqari memorized the Holy Qur'an at the ae of 13-14 after completing the primary school, he has moved to Jama'atu college of Arabic studies in Zaria for secondary education.He holds his first Bsc from the famous Al-Azhar University, Cairo in 1999. He also holds his master's degree in 2005,at Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria after that proceed to Bayero University Kano for his PHD in 2009 which he completed the doctorate degree in 2years.


  • In 1999 Prof Maqari start lecturing at Ahmad Bello University,Zaria. He was appointed as senior lecturer at Kaduna State University, Zaria. He was appointed as senior lecturer at KadunaState University in 2010. After spending almost onne year lecturing, Prof Maqari proceeds to Bayero University Kano where he served as a professor in Arabic and linguistics department, and retired in 2020.
